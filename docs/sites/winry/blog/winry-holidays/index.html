Winry on the Holidays

< Back Home

Winry hates dressing up, but it's cute, so we make her do it anyway, at least for a little while. On Halloween, she wears bat wings; she likes to pretend that these wings cause her to lose her balance, but she's just being dramatic. After all, right after putting on the wings, she's able to zoom across the floor to the opposite end of the apartment.

On Christmas, a valued tradition is for Winry to wear a Santa hat, even though she's on the naughty list. She hates this most of all, but is able to tolerate it in return for a Churu treat.
