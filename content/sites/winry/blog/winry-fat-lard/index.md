# Winry as a Fat Lard

[< Back Home](/sites/winry)

Winry is also a fat lard. She is the laziest cat that has ever existed on the planet. It is not uncommon for Winry to sit in the same spot ALL day. In fact, during her owners' recent weekend vacation, Winry was discovered to be sitting in the very same place that she was left in, just days before. She was laying in a pile of her own shed fur.

At least she's cute.
