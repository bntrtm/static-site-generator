# Meet Winry Millie Bainter


![a cat laying in the shape of a bread loaf](/images/loaf_baby.jpg)

Winry Millie Bainter was adopted from Melissa's Second Chances in November of 2022. At time of writing (May '25), she is four years old. Named after Winry Rockbell from Hiromu Arakawa's _Fullmetal Alchemist_, this fat cat spends her days lounging in the sun to photosynthesize enough energy to survive between her frequent meals. Her middle name, "Millie," comes from her shelter name.

## About Winry

Winry's favorite things include:

- food
- her owners
- ribbon toys
- water fountains
- watching birds and squirrels
- being lazy
- throwing up on the carpet
- hiding in closets

Winry's dislikes are:

- any noise outside The Walls
- strangers
- being brushed
- going 2 further nanoseconds than necessary without food

## Winry's Famous Quote

> "aeeeeeehhhh..."
> 
> -- Winry, asking for food
